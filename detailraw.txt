<section id="#gov">
  <section class="detail-head">
    <p>Help run the co-op itself.</p>
  </section>
  <section class="details d-none">
    <p>How it works</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Co-op members elect directors.</li>
      <li>Directors appoint officers, committees, and contractors.</li>
      <li>Directors budget and charge members monthly assessments to cover costs.</li>
      <li>Members vote on any changes in fundamental rules; directors vote on subsidiary operating rules.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>You can volunteer to be:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>One of the five directors of the corporation</li>
      <li>A corporate officer</li>
      <li>A member of a board-appointed committee</li>
      <li>A member of an informal committee or working group</li>
      <li>A one-person performer of good deeds</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
</section>
