More … Belong to a historic housing cooperative
  • Founded in 1960.
  • U.S.’s first-ever senior housing cooperative!
  • Self-governing “cooperative corporation” with 60 shareholders.
  • Each shareholder (“member”) occupies one apartment (“unit”).
  • For tax purposes, you are treated as the owner of your unit.
  • Some banks (e.g., National Cooperative Bank and First Republic) offer mortgages on co-op purchases.

How can I join?

  • Limited to households with at least one resident 55+
  • Limited to permanent residents
  • Asking price $375,000
  • Financing available from co-op lenders
  • Condition: As-is, except repainted in purchaser’s color
  • Purchase requires screening, interview, and co-op approval of membership
  • Disclosures will be available before you make a purchase offer
